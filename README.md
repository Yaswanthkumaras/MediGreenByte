# MediGreenByte
AI-Based Digital Waste and Carbon Footprint Analyzer for Healthcare Data Systems
# Problem Statement
The healthcare sector generates large amounts of digital data such as electronic health
records, medical images (MRI, CT scans), diagnostic reports, and patient monitoring
data. Hospitals store this data in cloud platforms and data centers to ensure
accessibility and long-term management. However, a significant amount of this data
becomes duplicate, inactive, or unnecessarily stored over time, leading to digital waste.
Maintaining such large volumes of data requires continuous server operations, which
increases energy consumption and carbon emissions in data centers.
Current healthcare systems mainly focus on data security and accessibility, but they
rarely consider the environmental impact of digital data storage. Therefore, there is a
need for a system that can identify digital waste and estimate the carbon footprint of
healthcare data systems.
# Proposed System
MediGreenByte is an AI-based platform that analyzes healthcare data storage and
identifies unnecessary or redundant digital data. The system examines different
healthcare datasets such as electronic health records, medical images, and archived
patient data. Using artificial intelligence, it detects duplicate files, inactive records, and
inefficient storage patterns.
The platform then estimates the digital carbon footprint caused by healthcare data
storage and displays the results through a dashboard. It also provides
recommendations such as removing redundant files, archiving inactive records, and
improving data storage efficiency. This system helps healthcare organizations reduce
digital waste, optimize data storage, and promote environmentally sustainable
healthcare IT systems

# Novelty of the Proposed System

The novelty of the proposed system lies in integrating artificial intelligence
with sustainable healthcare data management to address the
environmental impact of large-scale digital health infrastructure.
Unlike traditional healthcare information systems that focus primarily on
data storage and accessibility, MediGreenByte introduces a new
perspective by analyzing digital waste within healthcare data systems and
estimating its environmental impact.
